About Us

We’re guessing that you ended up here because a colleague or family member sent you a link or told you to check us out. That’s great! Word of Mouth really is powerful, huh?

Our Mission

Word of Mouth is designed by a therapist, for therapists. Our goal is to help clinicians expand their professional network and simplify the referral process. Our passion is seeing other therapists thrive, passing along business knowledge, and ensuring fast access to quality care for clients.

Our Story

After changing careers to follow his passion for psychology, our founder Tom discovered the roadblocks in starting and maintaining a private practice. He quickly noticed a trending problem for private practice owners: referrals. Colleagues seemed to spend the first half of their career searching for clients and the latter half trying to efficiently source them out to trusted colleagues. On top of that, therapists new to private practice often found it intimidating to learn about business and marketing while trying to build a caseload and see clients. Unable to find a service that addressed all of these needs, Tom decided to build a platform of his own, and Word of Mouth was born. His vision connects mental health professionals at all stages of their career on an easy-to-use digital network. Word of mouth eliminates the guesswork of starting a successful private practice and the frustrations of finding reliable therapists to recommend.

Meet our Founder, Tom

Tom lives in Shaker Heights, Ohio with his lovely wife and twin boys. He began his professional career in sales and marketing, but always dreamed of the day he could retire and become a therapist. It turns out retirement was too far off to pursue the life he wanted. In 2002, he returned to school and made his dream a reality. In 2022, Tom launched Word of Mouth to give therapists of all levels the tools and resources to achieve their practice goals, quickly place clients into quality care, and free up precious time.
